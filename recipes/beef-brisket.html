Beef Brisket

Easiest beef brisket there is

Great recipe to make the day before and reheat to serve.

Ingredients

  • Beef brisket
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Can of beer
  • Chilli sauce
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

  2. Season the brisket on all sides with salt and pepper, and place in a glass baking dish.

    Cover with a layer of sliced onions. In a medium bowl, mix together the beer, chili

    sauce, and brown sugar. Pour over the roast. Cover the dish tightly with aluminum foil.

  3. Bake for 3 hours in the preheated oven. Remove the aluminum foil, and bake for an

    additional 30 minutes. Let the brisket rest and cool slightly before slicing and

    returning to the dish. Reheat in the oven with the sauce spooned over the sliced meat.
